About this tool

This is an independent research project that collates publicly available information about UK grants and funding schemes relevant to property works. It is not a commercial service, not an advisory tool, and not affiliated with any scheme operator or government body.

Not advice. Nothing on this site constitutes financial, legal, tax, or professional advice of any kind. Grant eligibility, funding levels, and deadlines change regularly. The tool signposts you to official sources - it does not determine whether any scheme applies to your specific circumstances. Always verify current eligibility, funding levels, and deadlines directly on the official scheme page before committing to any works or expenditure.

Methodology

Selection criteria

Schemes are included if they meet all of the following criteria:

  • The scheme is publicly available - not restricted to closed procurement or invited bidders only.
  • An official source URL exists on a government, devolved administration, regulator, or statutory body website.
  • The scheme is relevant to property works (retrofit, adaptation, heritage repair, empty homes, or affordable housing supply).
  • The scheme is currently open, recently closed (with successors noted), or in a confirmed pipeline.

Schemes are excluded if they are purely commercial products (e.g. green mortgages), tied to a specific installer or supplier relationship, or if no reliable official source URL can be confirmed.

Verification cycle

Each scheme entry records a lastVerified date in YYYY-MM format. The aim is to review all entries on a quarterly cycle, checking:

  • Whether the scheme is still open and accepting applications.
  • Whether funding levels, deadlines, or eligibility criteria have changed.
  • Whether the official URL remains valid.

Entries with a verify: true flag contain one or more fields that could not be confirmed directly from the official source at the time of writing and should be treated with additional caution.

What this tool does not do

  • It does not collect or store any personal data entered into the wizard.
  • It does not contact scheme operators on your behalf.
  • It does not recommend specific installers, advisers, or intermediaries.
  • It does not make any determination of eligibility.
